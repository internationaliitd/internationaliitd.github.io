International Programs - IIT Delhi

Incoming Exchange Students

We offer a distinguished student exchange program at IIT Delhi, and it can be a pivotal part of your undergraduate life. Student exchange is an edifying experience for us as well, given the distinctive inputs visiting students bring from their current universities, and we are always delighted to include people from diverse backgrounds in our academic community.

To efficiently share resources and remain at the forefront on the scientific and technological developments, IITD has MOUs (Memorandum of Understanding) signed with major national and international institutions, which facilitate engagement in various areas including student and faculty exchange, joint research and fellowships etc. UG, PG, doctoral and post-doctoral levels.

The Undergraduate Student Exchange Program enables currently enrolled students interested in taking classes at IITD that are not offered at their home university. Students can customize their course of study with options for part-time or full-time enrolment and the ability to stay for one or two academic terms. The student also has the flexibility to choose courses outside of his/her department, helping him get a wider exposure.

IIT Delhi provides you with the advantage of staying in the heart of the capital city of India, which comes with a host of benefits, including witnessing major national events like Republic Day, Independence Day among others, and the convenience of visiting and seeking help from your countryâ€™s embassy in Delhi. In addition to enrolment in courses at the college, you can explore many of our monuments, laboratories, theatres, and extracurricular activities, all in the rich cultural and historical setting of New Delhi.
